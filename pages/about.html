About

Alexander Brightman is a musician and producer from Newcastle, Australia.

He plays guitar, bass, keyboard/synthesizer, and he sings. He records and produces his own music, and produces and publishes video content on social media.

His debut release Lunar Vertigo (EP) is available on Spotify, and with future releases on the horizon you'll want to follow him on Instagram so that you don't miss out.

The music of Alexander Brightman is a cosmic journey through ethereal time and space, with a dash of psychedelia and an alternative rock edge.

Website, images, and music produced by Alexander Brightman.
